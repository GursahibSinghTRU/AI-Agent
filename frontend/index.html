About Risk & Safety at TRU

We provide comprehensive support, training, and resources to ensure a safe and secure campus community.

Emergency Response

Our emergency procedures ensure rapid response to any incident. Trained personnel are always on standby to assist in emergency situations.

Training & Education

We offer mandatory safety training, workplace ergonomics, WHMIS certification, and emergency procedures training for all staff and students.

Incident Reporting

Easy and confidential incident reporting systems allow us to track, investigate, and prevent recurring safety issues across campus.

Campus Alerts

Real-time safety alerts and notifications keep the TRU community informed of any potential threats or urgent situations.

Mental Health Support

Comprehensive mental health and wellness resources available to students and staff, including counselling and crisis support.

Accessibility

We ensure all facilities meet accessibility standards and provide accommodations for individuals with disabilities.

Our Services

Comprehensive risk and safety support tailored to meet the needs of our campus community.

Workplace Safety Inspections

Regular inspections of all campus facilities to ensure compliance with health and safety regulations. Report hazards immediately to maintain a safe environment.

Sexualized Violence Prevention

Resources, training, and support for survivors of sexualized violence. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy and provide confidential reporting options.

Hazard Communication

WHMIS training and access to safety data sheets for all hazardous materials used on campus. Employees working with chemicals must complete certification.

Emergency Procedures

Evacuation drills, first aid training, and emergency response protocols. Know your assembly points and follow posted emergency procedures.

Policy Development

Evidence-based safety policies reflecting best practices in higher education. All policies are regularly reviewed and updated.

Conflict Resolution

Mediation and conflict resolution services to maintain a respectful workplace. Report conflicts to HR or through anonymous channels.

Access important policies, training, and support information. Use the chatbot to get quick answers to your safety questions.
