# Risk & Safety Services - Resources & Support

Thompson Rivers University's Risk and Safety Services are here to encourage and facilitate robust risk management practices and support the development of a safe workplace and learning environment.

We have the resources, professionalism and skills to support our community by integrating risk management processes across all university functions. Our approach is collegial, creative and collaborative, allowing us to create a more engaged health and safety culture throughout TRU.

## Services & Resources Offered

### Insurance & Claims Management
- Volunteers and property coverage
- Driving and student insurance
- Travelling support
- Contract review
- Claim reporting and support

### Workplace Safety & Health
- Incident reporting
- Safe work procedures
- Safety training and orientation
- Ergonomics support
- Biosafety guidance
- Safety resources

### Security & Emergency Services
- Emergency procedures coordination
- Security patrols and support
- First aid services
- Safe walk programs
- Personal safety resources
- Access control management
- Security camera systems
- Lost and found services

### Facilities & Space Management
- University space booking
- General room bookings
- Table reservations
- Outdoor event bookings
- Filming, photography and drone use on campus
