# Winter Sport & Recreation Safety Resources

**Region:** Northern BC (Northern Health)
**Source:** Northern Health Official Website
**Context:** Winter weather safety for public and recreational activities.

## Key Safety Partners & Resources
- **Avalanche Safety:** Avalanche Canada (Interactive map of forecasts and advisories)
- **Concussion Prevention:** Hockey Canada (Concussion Prevention Resource Centre)
- **Injury Prevention:** BC Injury Research and Prevention Unit (BCIRPU) / Active and Safe Central
- **Outdoor Safety:** Parachute, AdventureSmart
- **Sports Medicine:** SportMedBC (Resource library)
- **Youth Specific:** Canadian Paediatric Society (Skiing and snowboarding safety tips)
- **Policy/Guidance:** Government of BC (Sport safety)

## Emergency & Operational Status
- **Emergency Departments:** May be closed due to temporary service interruptions.
- **Required Action:** Check Emergency Department status prior to travel or seeking medical care.
- **Service Availability:** Verify ED status before initiating winter travel plans.
