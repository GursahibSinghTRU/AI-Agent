# Sun Peaks Resort: Summer Safety & Risk Awareness

## Emergency Contact
- **On-Mountain Emergency:** Call Patrol/Dispatch at **250.578.5521**.

## Legal & Liability
- **Waivers:** Guests must review and sign release agreements/activity waivers before participation.
- **Terms:** Exclusion of liability and assumption of risk apply based on waiver terms.

## Activity Specific Safety
### Bike Park
- **Trail Markings:** Orange oval markers designate freeride trails with man-made features requiring jumping skills.
- **Equipment:** Use appropriate bike types and protective gear.
- **Conduct:** Adhere to code of conduct and show courtesy to others.

### Lifts
- **Equipment Status:** Chairlifts are heavy machinery.
- **Protocol:** Follow specific safety rules for loading, riding, and unloading.

### Hiking
- **Environment:** Safety tips and rules apply to alpine hiking areas.

## Environmental Hazards
- **Wildlife:** Black bears are free-roaming; guests must remain aware of animal presence.
- **Fire Risk:** Resort measures exist to minimize interface and structural fire risks; exercise extreme caution.

## General Safety
- **Risk Mitigation:** Common sense and personal awareness are required to reduce risk elements.
