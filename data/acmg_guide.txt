# Association of Canadian Mountain Guides (ACMG)

## Organization Profile
- **Mission:** Professional association for trained and certified mountain guides and instructors in Canada.
- **History:** Established 1963; first non-Europe guiding association recognized by IFMGA.
- **Focus:** Upholding safety standards, professionalism, and advancing the profession of mountain guiding.

## Standards and Certification
- **Training Model:** Blended delivery (online, in-class, field-based) adhering to ACMG/IFMGA high standards.
- **Programs:** Mountain, Alpine, Ski, Rock, Hiking, Via Ferrata Guides; Climbing Instructors.
- **Compliance:** Members must adhere to ACMG Code of Conduct and Scope of Practice.
- **Assessment:** Practical field-based assessment criteria required for certification.

## Safety and Risk Resources
- **Liability:** Access to EQUA/ACMG Liability Insurance for members.
- **Risk Governance:** Dedicated documents covering Risk and Responsibility and Governance.
- **Specialized Safety Roles:** Members operate in mountain rescue, avalanche research, and forecasting.
- **Wellness:** Mental Health Resources and Health and Wellness Insurance available.

## Governance and Committees
- **Key Committees:** Conduct Review, Mental Health, Financial Review, Land Access, Professional Practice Audit.
- **Transparency:** Organizational chart and Board of Directors information available.
- **Events:** 2026 Spring Summit (Penticton, B.C., May 7-10) includes AGM and CPD sessions.
- **Access:** Land Access Inquiry Form and InfoEx Access Program available for permit management.
