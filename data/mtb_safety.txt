# Revelstoke Mountain Resort (RMR) - Mountain Biking Safety Policy

## Emergency Contact
- **Patrol/Emergency:** 250.814.5055
- **Accident Reporting:** Identify yourself immediately if involved in or witness a collision to bike patrol, staff, or local authority.

## Access & Entry Requirements
- **Credentials:** Valid resort pass and completed waiver mandatory.
- **Trail Privileges:** Bike Park privileges may be revoked for breach of the Responsibility Code.
- **Foot Traffic:** Prohibited within the Bike Park, with exception of Stoke Climb.
- **Uphill Travel:** Restricted to designated up-track (Stoke Climb) only.

## Skill Level & Eligibility
- **Lift-Accessed Downhill Trails:** Require minimum intermediate to advanced riding skills.
- **Trail Selection:** Do not attempt advanced trails without necessary skills, experience, and gear.
- **Rider Control:** Must be able to stop and avoid other people or objects.
- **Impairment:** Riding prohibited if judgment is impaired by drugs, alcohol, or fatigue.

## Protective Equipment (PPE)
- **Helmets:** Mandatory on all trails within Bike Parks.
- **Full-Face Helmets:** Strongly recommended for all downhill trails.
- **Additional Gear:** Strongly recommended for beginners (gloves, pads, sturdy footwear).
- **Condition:** Helmets must be undamaged and fit securely.
- **Rental Availability:** Protective gear available to rent or purchase at resort shops.

## Equipment Inspection Checklist
Ensure bike is in safe working order before riding:
- **Structure:** Frame free of cracks, damage, or dents.
- **Mechanical:** Front/rear brakes adequate, suspension functional, bolts tight.
- **Components:** Handlebars/grips secured, seat fastened securely.
- **Tires:** Adequate tread and appropriate inflation pressure.
- **Assistance:** Consult qualified mechanic if unsure about inspection.

## Minor Supervision Guidelines
- **RMR Policy:** Does not provide rider supervision.
- **Age 7 and under:** Must be accompanied by a responsible adult at all times.
- **Age 8 to 10:** Must be supervised by a responsible individual aged 13 or older.
- **Parental Responsibility:** Parents/guardians are responsible for their children.

## Mountain Biker's Responsibility Code
- **Awareness:** Be safety conscious; stay on marked trails; avoid cut switchbacks.
- **Ride Right:** Yield to other riders when entering a trail.
- **Visibility:** Do not stop where you obstruct a trail or are not visible from above.
- **Trail Care:** Inspect trails/features before use and throughout the day.
- **Lift Safety:** Ensure physical dexterity/ability to load/unload lifts safely.
- **Cooperation:** Report accidents/collisions to staff.
