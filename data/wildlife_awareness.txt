## BC Forest Safety Council: Wildlife & Bear Safety Awareness

**Purpose**
Guide for forestry workers to avoid bear conflicts, ensure crew safety, and maintain healthy wildlife populations.

**Key Safety Practices**
*   **Education:** Proper education and safe work practices are critical.
*   **Identification:** Recognize bear signs and species.
*   **Prevention:** Avoid surprise encounters.
*   **Management:** Manage interactions and minimize threats from problem bears.

**Disclaimer**
BCFSC guidance does not imply or guarantee compliance for specific company situations.

**Downloadable Resources**
*   **Bear Safety Training Program Resources** (PDF, 821KB): Tool for training programs, internal safety memos, and crew education.
*   **Bear Spray vs. Bullets** (PDF, 50KB): US Fish and Wildlife Service fact sheet comparing protection methods.
*   **Get Bear Smart for Tree Planters** (PDF, 138KB): Specific safety pamphlet for tree planters.
