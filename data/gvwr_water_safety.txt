# Water Safety Guidelines

## Supervision & Attention
*   **Constant Supervision:** An adult must watch children at all times near or in water; flotation devices are not a substitute for supervision.
*   **Bathtub Safety:** Never leave a child unattended in a bathtub; drowning can occur in as little as two inches of water.
*   **Pool Security:** Install proper fencing and childproof gates to prevent unsupervised access to swimming pools.
*   **Current Awareness:** Exercise extreme caution near ocean strong currents; be aware that being swept away can happen instantly.

## Equipment & Gear
*   **Personal Flotation Devices (PFDs):** Non-swimmers must wear a PFD near or in the water.
*   **Life Jacket Fit:** Lifejackets must have correct weight limits and fit snugly. They should not ride above the ears.

## Skills & Education
*   **Swimming Competency:** Learning to swim is a critical life skill; encourage all family members to learn.
*   **Diving Safety:** Prohibit head-first diving unless the individual is trained and the water is deep enough.
*   **First Aid:** Be proactive in obtaining First Aid and CPR training.
*   **Behavioral Safety:** Educate children on water dangers and ensure they play with care to avoid accidents like dunking games.
