# Banff National Park: Mountain Biking Safety & Regulations

**Season & Scope**
- **Operating Period:** May to October.
- **Infrastructure:** 360+ km of trails available.
- **Skill Levels:** Ranges from easy to difficult.

**Activity Restrictions**
- **Prohibited in Park:** Freeriding and downhilling are discouraged.
- **Designated High-Risk Zones:** Direct users to areas for downhill/freeriding:
  - Golden, BC
  - Fernie, BC
  - Canada Olympic Park, Calgary

**Safety Requirements**
- **Hazard Awareness:** Consult Trail Condition Reports prior to riding.
- **Compliance:** Adhere to all rules, regulations, and trail etiquette.
- **Specific Gear:** Guidelines provided for E-bikes and Fat bikes.
- **Environment:** Riders must maintain pristine natural environment (wildlife and people safety).

**Resource Locations**
- **Sections:** Banff Area, Lake Louise Area.
- **Source:** Government of Canada (Date Modified: 2025-09-17).
