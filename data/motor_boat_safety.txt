### Water Vessel Safety Guidelines (Motorized & Non-Motorized)

**Core Safety Protocols**
- **Personal Flotation Devices (PFDs):** Mandatory for all occupants on all vessels. Must be properly fitted.
- **Substance Use:** Operating vessels under the influence of alcohol or drugs is illegal and strictly prohibited.
- **Weather Monitoring:** Check forecasts before and during trips.
- **Pre-Departure Checklist:** Conduct safety checks and file a float plan with a contact ashore including route and estimated return time.
- **Vessel Limits:** Do not exceed vessel capacity or operational limits.

**Required Safety Equipment**
- **Motorized Vessels:** Fire extinguisher, flares, towing gear, radio, first aid kit, life jackets.
- **Non-Motorized Vessels (Kayak/SUP):** Life jackets, whistle, bailer, tow rope.

**Licensing & Training**
- **Motorized:** Pleasure Craft Operator Card (PCOC) via Boating BC is required for all operators.
- **Non-Motorized:** Operator certification is recommended for navigation safety.
- **Recommended Courses:** BoaterSmart! (Boating) and PaddlerSmart! (Paddling).

**Reference Authority**
- Transport Canada Safe Boating Guide.
