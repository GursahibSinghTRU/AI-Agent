# Avalanche Safety Fundamentals

**Definition**
- An avalanche is a mass of snow moving down a slope.

**Risk Profile (US Data)**
- Annual Fatalities: 25-30 deaths per winter.
- Annual Injuries: Numerous non-fatal incidents reported.

**Core Safety Requirements**
Safe backcountry operations require adherence to the "Three G's":
1. Get the Forecast
2. Get the Gear
3. Get the Training

**Education Philosophy**
- Avalanche education is a life-long process.
- Knowledge enables decision-making regarding timing, location, and method of backcountry travel.
