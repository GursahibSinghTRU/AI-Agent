# BC Bike & E-bike Safety & Regulations (Risk & Safety Knowledge Base)

## General Requirements
- **Road Rules:** Cyclists must obey all traffic signs, signals, and lights; yield to pedestrians.
- **Roadside Position:** Ride as far right as practicable on paved roadways. Sidewalk riding prohibited unless signed otherwise.
- **Formation:** Ride single file; do not ride beside another device unless passing.
- **Communication:** Use hand signals for turns, slowing, or stopping.
- **Visibility:** Mandatory white front light and red rear reflector/light between sunset and sunrise. High-visibility clothing recommended.
- **Impaired Riding:** Prohibited while under the influence of alcohol/drugs or distracted by electronic devices (e.g., cellphones).
- **Safety:** Watch for obstacles such as potholes, debris, and rumble strips.

## Helmet Regulations
- **Requirement:** Mandatory for all ages when riding on public roads.
- **Supervision:** Parents/guardians must ensure children under 16 wear helmets.
- **Standards:** Helmets must meet *Bicycle Safety Helmet Standards Regulation*.
- **Penalties:** Violation may result in a $29 fine (subject to exemption regulations).

## Restricted Highways
Cycling is prohibited on specific segments of the following provincial highways unless signage permits otherwise:
- **Highway 1:** Specific segments in Hope, Abbotsford, Kamloops (e.g., Coquihalla Highway junction).
- **Highway 3:** Hope to Coquihalla Highway junction.
- **Highway 5:** Coquihalla Highway segment between Hope and Kamloops.
- **Highway 19:** Segments south of Parksville and around Nanaimo.
- **Highway 91 & 91A:** Specific approach routes to Annacis and Queensborough Bridges.
- **Highway 97C:** Junction with Highway 5A to Highway 97.
- **Highway 99:** 1st Avenue in Surrey to Oak Street Bridge approach.
- **Note:** Check DriveBC for closures due to weather or construction.

## Prohibited Bridges & Tunnels
Cyclists are prohibited from riding on the road portion or over/through:
- **Tunnels:** George Massey Tunnel, Cassiar Connector Tunnel, Port Mann Bridge approaches.
- **Bridges:** Ironworkers Memorial Second Narrows Bridge, Oak Street Bridge, Knight Street Bridge, William R. Bennett Bridge, Annacis Bridge, East Channel Bridge, Queensborough Bridge, Pattullo Bridge.
- **Permitted:** Nelson Bridge (Highway 3A) and Agassiz-Rosedale Bridge (narrow roads apply).
- **Instruction:** Use designated shuttles where required (e.g., George Massey Tunnel).

## Legislation & Resources
- **Key Laws:** Motor Vehicle Act, Traffic Regulations, Low-Powered Vehicles.
- **Specific Regulations:** Bicycle Safety Helmets Regulation, Bicycle Safety Helmet Exemption Regulation.
- **Resources:** DriveBC (conditions/weather), BC Cycling Coalition (Bike Sense), BC Parks (Cycling), Municipal rules for shared paths.
- **Transit:** Contact TransLink or BC Transit for bike/e-bike transit restrictions.
