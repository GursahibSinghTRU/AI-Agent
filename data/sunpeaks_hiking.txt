# Sun Peaks Resort: Hiking Safety & Risk Protocols

## General Hiking Protocols
- **Never Hike Alone:** Solo hiking is prohibited; partners or groups required.
- **Trip Reporting:** Inform someone outside the group of hiking plans and expected return times.
- **Weather Awareness:** Mountain temperatures are cooler and change rapidly; plan and dress accordingly.
- **Navigation:** Carry a physical trail map or save it electronically on a mobile device.
- **Trail Adherence:** Remain on marked hiking trails to protect the alpine ecosystem.
- **Restricted Areas:** Hiking is strictly prohibited on Bike Park trails.

## Emergency & Communication
- **Emergency Whistle:** Always pack a whistle.
- **Distress Signal:** Three short whistle blasts indicate trouble and require assistance.
- **Situational Awareness:** Continuously scan surroundings and note landmarks for navigation.

## Hazard Mitigation & Prohibitions
- **Wildlife Interaction:** Never approach or feed animals. Make plenty of noise to avoid surprising wildlife.
- **Smoke-Free Policy:** Smoking of all types (tobacco, vaping, recreational/prescribed cannabis) is prohibited on the mountain, including lift lines, chairlifts, ski runs, Nordic trails, bike/hike trails, and the Sunburst Lodge.
- **Drone Policy:** Operation of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)/drones is prohibited for the general public and hobbyists for safety and privacy reasons.
- **Essential Gear:** Carry water, food, sunscreen, watch, bug spray, and emergency whistle.
- **Lodging/Access:** Refer to Summer Chairlift Safety page for lift loading/unloading protocols.
