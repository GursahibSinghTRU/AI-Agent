# Canadian Boating Safety Equipment Requirements (Summary)

## Regulatory Basis
Equipment requirements align with:
*   Small Vessel Regulations (SOR/2010-91)
*   Navigation Safety Regulations (SOR/2020-216)
*   Collision Regulations (C.R.C., c. 1416)

## General Equipment Categories
*   **Personal Lifesaving:** PFD for each person, buoyant heaving line (min 15m), reboarding device (if freeboard > 0.5m).
*   **Vessel Safety:** Manual propelling device or anchor, bailer/bilge pump.
*   **Visual Signals:** Watertight flashlight, distress pyrotechnics (count varies by size), dive flags (if required).
*   **Navigation:** Sound-signalling device, navigation lights, magnetic compass, radar reflector.
*   **Fire Fighting:** Extinguishers (BC rating varies), fire pump/hose (vessels >24m), axes, buckets.

## Requirements by Vessel Size & Type

### 1. Human-Powered Pleasure Craft
*   *Exceptions:* Paddleboats, watercycles, stand-up paddleboards, sealed-hull sit-on-top kayaks require only PFD, sound device, and watertight flashlight.
*   *Standard:* PFD, heaving line, reboarding device, bailer, flashlight, 6 distress signals (unless <1nm from shore), sound device, navigation lights.

### 2. Power-Driven and Sailing Vessels
| Length | Distress Signals | Anchor Length | Fire Extinguisher | Additional Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Up to 6m** | 3 (non-smoke) | 15m | 5BC (if engine/fuel/galley) | Compass exempt if <8m/daylight |
| **6m – 9m** | 6 (non-smoke) | 15m | 5BC (if engine/fuel/galley) | Same as above |
| **9m – 12m** | 12 (max 6 smoke) | 30m | 10BC (if engine/fuel/galley) | Compass required |
| **12m – 24m** | 12 (max 6 smoke) | 50m | 10BC (Fuel/Accommodation/Machinery) | 1 or 2 Sound appliances (+Bell if >20m). 1 Axe, 2 Buckets (10+L). |
| **Over 24m** | 12 (max 6 smoke) | 50m | 10BC (Fuel/Accommodation/Machinery) | SOLAS Lifebuoy (lighted + line). 2 Axes, 4 Buckets (10+L). Fire pump required. |

## Conditions & Exceptions
*   **Navigation Lights:** Required only after sunset, before sunrise, or in restricted visibility (fog, snow).
*   **Radar Reflectors:** Required on non-metallic vessels under 20m (unless impractical or safe in limited traffic/daylight).
*   **Competition Exemption:** Vessels may be exempt from equipment requirements if participating in a race with accompanying vessels carrying appropriate gear.
*   **Location Exemption:** Some equipment (e.g., pyrotechnic signals) exempt if vessel never exceeds 1 nautical mile from shore.
*   **Bilge Pump:** Not required for vessels incapable of capsizing or with watertight sealed compartments.
*   **PFD Sizing:** Must be appropriate size for each person on board.
