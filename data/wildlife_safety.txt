# Wildlife Safety Guidelines (AdventureSmart)

## Bear Safety
**Habitat:** Black bears (12 provinces/territories); Grizzly bears (Western provinces, Northern territories).
**Precautions:** Research local activity; check with park wardens/wildlife officers.
**Behavior:** Alert bears to presence (sing/talk calmly). Steer clear if sighted. Do not feed bears. Pack out waste.
**Encounter Response:** Remain still/calm. Do not run. Use deterrent. Slowly back away.
**Required Gear:** Bear-resistant food canister; deterrent (spray, flare, horn); binoculars; backpack to cover head/neck.

## Cougar Safety
**Habitat:** Southwest Alberta, lower BC (endangered elsewhere).
**Precautions:** Hike in groups; carry strong walking stick. Watch for tracks.
**Behavior:** Alert presence. If sighted, steer clear.
**Encounter Response:** Stand tall, back away slowly, maintain eye contact, give escape route. Never turn back or run. If aggressive, appear larger; if attacked, fight back.
**Reporting:** Contact Conservation Officer Service immediately after confrontation.
**Required Gear:** Sturdy walking stick; binoculars.
