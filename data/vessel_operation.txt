## Human-Powered Watercraft Operational Safety (Transport Canada)

**Scope**
Applies to human-powered pleasure craft including kayaks, canoes, paddleboards, rowboats, and sailboats.

**Competency Requirements**
- **Unpowered:** Proof of competency is not required.
- **Fitted with Motor:** Proof of competency is required if a motor is installed, even if the motor is turned off.

**Safety Equipment**
- Mandatory safety equipment required per *Small Vessel Regulations* and *Safe Boating Guide*.
- **Pre-Trip Action:** Schedule a Pleasure Craft Courtesy Check before first use.

**Visibility and Signaling**
- **Vessel Color:** Use bright colors (red, yellow, orange) as unpowered vessels are difficult to spot.
- **Signaling:** Keep signalling devices within hand’s reach for emergency use.

**Environmental Awareness**
- Continuously monitor conditions: water temperature, tides, currents, wind, and maritime traffic.
- Special attention required for sea kayaking due to increased environmental hazards.
